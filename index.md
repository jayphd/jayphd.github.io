<p> Hi, I am a postdoctoral research associate in <a href="https://efmlab.nd.edu/">Environmetal Fluid Mechanics Laboratory</a>
at the University of Notre Dame. I am working on the <a href="https://efmlab.nd.edu/research/miso-bob/"> MISO-BOB project</a> investigating the interactions between monsoonal storms and ocean thermodynamics. I am also a visiting scholar at the School of Earth and Environment, University of Leeds where I study the orographic influence of the Western Ghats on the Indian monsoon [ <a  href="https://rmets.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/qj.4367/"> Phadtare et al. 2022</a>]. <br> </p>
  
<b> Research Interest: </b> <br>
I primarily study mesoscale weather phenomena, viz., mountain-valley/land-sea breezes, boundary layer processes, mesoscale convective systems, cyclonic storms,  using observations and numerical modeling.
